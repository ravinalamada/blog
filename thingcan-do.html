Mahanoro

Mahanoro is a good place to live or to do vacation as life here is cheaper rather than the other places in the coast of Madagascar. Endeed, if you love swimming because the sea is mostly fine.

The thing you can do there:

  1. At the weekend

    You can do a picnic in Ambatolatabatra with friends or family.It is best place for picnic in Mahanoro as it is calm and spacious. what is more, there are natural rocks on the side of the sea, which has shape of a table,which makes it really appealling because it is good for sitting.

  2. Want to eat out?

If you are keen on eating out,mon desire is the best restaurant in Mahanoro. It has a wounderful atmosphere and the food is so crazy as well.
